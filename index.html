About me

I am an experienced Software Engineer with 9+ years of experience in designing and developing complex systems. Worked as a Development Engineer at FDTech GmbH, contributing to advanced driver assistance and autonomous driving solutions. During this time I have developed strong expertise in Python and a little in C++ which supports my daily work. My experience also includes system integration, analytics, and building scalable software in agile environments.

An academic foundation in Electronics Engineering was established at the University of Mumbai. This was followed by a Master's degree in Embedded Systems from Technische Universität, Chemnitz. The background provides me with a strong understanding of low-level systems, real-time computing, and hardware-software interaction. Early exposure to Deep Learning came through research work at TU Chemnitz. This experience was further strengthened during a master's thesis at BASELABS, focusing on LiDAR data processing and neural networks for object detection in automotive applications.

In the recent years, out of curiosity, I have been growing my focus on DevOps practices and it has shaped my recent learning goals. Hands-on experience was developed during building CI/CD pipelines from scratch using GitHub Actions and Jenkins over different projects. Strong proficiency in Linux environments and Bash scripting support the automation capabilities. CI, CD as well as CT workflows were managed using Git, with an emphasis on reliability and reproducibility. The main goal is to improve software delivery efficiency through automation.

Outside of professional work, I also enjoy a personal homelab that runs on a Raspberry Pi 4. This was developed interest from taking control of own data rather than feeding it to AI algorithms from large companies. Multiple services such as Pi-hole, Home Assistant, Joplin, Karakeep, Dockhand, Portainer, and Immich are being self-hosted and maintained. This setup ensures full control over personal data and reduces reliance on external cloud providers. Also, it serves as a continuous learning and experimentation environment, without risking any downtime in enterprise systems.
